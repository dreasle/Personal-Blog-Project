Hi, I'm Andy, Maker of Mona Lucy Makes.

I live in Seattle, WA and love to sew and make things. My love for creating came from my mother, the ultimate maker of things. She ran a ceramics business out of our house and was always a creative force as I was growing up.

I rediscovered sewing after college when a friend of mine opened a lovely boutique consigment shop and asked me to create some things to sell there. It all started with a simple little blue shirt and some very popular a-line skirts. After a few years, my focus turned to my career and then family but as my son entered school, I rediscovered sewing a second time.

This time around I thought I would explore the world of independant pattern makers and fellow sewists online and start sewing for myself. And maybe family... and a few friends... who am I kidding, I love making things for other people :)

But I'm starting off sewing for myself and will share my adventures here.

Thanks for stopping by!
